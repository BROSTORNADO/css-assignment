    • Lionel Messi

    Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who is widely considered one of the greatest players of all time. He was born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina. Messi spent the majority of his career playing for FC Barcelona, where he won numerous trophies, including ten La Liga titles and four UEFA Champions League titles.

    Messi's extraordinary dribbling skills, vision, and goal-scoring ability have earned him numerous individual awards, including seven Ballon d'Or titles. He is known for his humility and sportsmanship both on and off the field, making him an admired figure worldwide.

    In August 2021, Messi left Barcelona due to financial difficulties faced by the club and joined Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) in France, continuing to mesmerize fans with his exceptional talent.

    Angel Di Maria

    Angel Di Maria is an Argentine professional footballer known for his versatility and flair on the field. He was born on February 14, 1988, in Rosario, Argentina. Di Maria has had a successful career playing for top clubs such as Benfica, Real Madrid, Manchester United, and Paris Saint-Germain (PSG).

    Di Maria is admired for his dribbling skills, precise passing, and powerful shots. He has won numerous league titles and a UEFA Champions League trophy during his career.

    Di Maria has also been a key player for the Argentine national team, contributing to their success in international competitions, including the Copa America.

    Enzo Fernandez

    Enzo Fernandez, full name Enzo Nicolas Fernandez, is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a midfielder. He was born on May 24, 1996, in Lyon, France, and is the son of former footballer and current coach Marcelo Gallardo.

    Fernandez began his professional career at River Plate in Argentina and later played for clubs such as Real Madrid B and Deportivo Alaves in Spain.

    He is known for his technical ability, passing, and work rate on the pitch. Fernandez has represented the Argentina U20 national team in the past and continues to impress with his performances at both the club and international level.
